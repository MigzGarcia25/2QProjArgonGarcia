Plastic Memories

Plastic Memories is a Japanese light novel which was adapted into an Anime in 2015. The show takes place in the future. There are two types of living humans in this world. They are the humans, and giftias. The only difference between them is their lifespan. Humans live about 80 years on average, while as giftias live approximately 81,920 until they are retrieved. If these giftias aren't retrieved by the time of their expiration date, these giftias will turn into wanderers which wrecks the place. Isla along with her partner Tsukasa, spends her last days on the Terminal Service.

Angel Beats

Angel Beats is an original anime series created by the legendary Jun Maeda. The story takes place in a school in the afterlife where in deceased high schoolers are to spend their lives in. until they become reincarnated, which they are trying to prevent. Otonashi Yuzuru, a boy who didn't know of his own death joins an Anti-Angel group in school. The purpose of this group is to assassinated angel, who we may also refer to as Tachibana Kanade.

The Day I Became A God

Kamisama ni Natta Hi also known as "The Day I Became A God" is an original anime series created by the legendary Jun Maeda. The story is about a young boy named Narukami Youta who discovers a young girl who claims to be a God. This girl, Hina Sato, said that the world will end in thirty days. Her omnipotent powers were proved after various events which causes Narukami to believe.
